## Recommended Study Path

1. **Benchmark** — take the [official free practice assessment](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/exams/az-900/practice/assessment?assessment-type=practice&assessmentId=23) to see where you stand
2. **Read** — work through the 3 [Microsoft Learn learning paths](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/azure-fundamentals/) as your primary reference
3. **Watch** — reinforce with [John Savill's playlist](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLlVtbbG169nED0_vMEniWBQjSoxTsBYS3) or [freeCodeCamp's 8-hour course](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5abffC-K40c)
4. **Practise** — use the [quiz CLI](../quiz/README.md) in this repo, then move to [Tutorials Dojo](https://portal.tutorialsdojo.com/courses/free-az-900-microsoft-azure-fundamentals-practice-exams-sampler/) and [ExamTopics](https://www.examtopics.com/exams/microsoft/az-900/view/) for variety
5. **Hands-on** — work through the [labs in this repo](../guides/labs/) against a real [Azure free account](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/)
6. **Final check** — re-run the practice assessment and aim for 80%+ before booking the real exam
